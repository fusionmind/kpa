BCBA Certification Application Instructions

Instructions

  1. Review the current BCBA requirements to ensure that you meet the requirements before applying.
    • Your application will expire 90 days after payment is submitted. At that point, you will need to submit a new application and pay another application fee.
  2. Select the application type below.
  3. Complete this application, including submitting the non-refundable fee.
  4. Submit additional documents to the BACB at the address below, including:
    • Official transcript(s) for your degree and coursework
    • If not submitted in the application, your Final Experience Verification Form(s)
  5. Once all documents have been received, allow two weeks for processing.
